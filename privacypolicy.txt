Privacy Policy for Timestamp App

Last Updated: 24-Feb-2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Timestamp App.

Information We Collect:
- Timestamps created when you press buttons in the app
- Basic device information required by Android

How We Use Your Information:
- All timestamp data is stored locally on your device
- Data is only shared when you choose to send it via email
- We do not collect or transmit your data to any servers

Data Storage and Security:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- You can delete all data using the Reset Counter button

Third-Party Services:
- This app does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services

Your Rights:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete all stored timestamps using the Reset function

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact jeff.r.parent@gmail.com.
